❓ Student FAQ
Do I need programming experience?
Nope. Our events are beginner-friendly with dedicated tracks and mentors to help you learn fast.

How much does it cost?
Nothing. Maximally hackathons are 100% free for all students, always.

Can I join solo?
Yes. Come with a team or join solo — we’ll help you squad up at the start of every event.

What if I don’t have an idea?
No stress. Every hackathon has challenge prompts and optional tracks to help spark ideas.

Do I need to stay up the whole time?
Definitely not. Build at your own pace. Sleep is cool. Rested minds build better.
